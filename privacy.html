Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Apex Talent Group ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you interact with our talent matching services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Public profile information from professional networks and portfolio sites where individuals have made their information publicly available
  • Contact information (email, professional handles) for outreach with explicit opt-in consent
  • Career background for matching purposes (skills, experience, role preferences)
  • Communications with us, including email replies and consent confirmations

2. How We Use Information

  • To match candidates with relevant job opportunities
  • To facilitate introductions between candidates and hiring companies (only with consent)
  • To send relevant career opportunities via email
  • To improve our matching algorithms and service quality

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Candidate information is only shared with potential employers after the candidate provides explicit consent (e.g., replying "yes" to our outreach email). All sharing is purpose-limited to the specific opportunity discussed.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of all communications by replying "STOP" or "UNSUBSCRIBE"
  • Withdraw previously given consent at any time

5. LinkedIn Integration

Our use of information received from LinkedIn APIs adheres to LinkedIn's API Terms of Use and developer policies. We use LinkedIn integration solely to share job opportunities on our company page and to facilitate professional introductions. We do not sell or transfer LinkedIn data to third parties.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect data, including encrypted storage, secure API access, and limited internal access controls. Sensitive financial information (W-9 forms, payment details) is only shared via secure channels with verified corporate contacts.

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our website does not use tracking cookies, analytics, or third-party advertising. No personal data is collected from website visitors who do not contact us directly.

8. International Users

We operate globally and may process data from individuals in any country. By contacting us, you consent to data processing in the United States and other countries where we operate, subject to applicable privacy laws.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
